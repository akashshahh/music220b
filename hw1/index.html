Sound Logo

A 4-voice sawtooth oscillator chord progression with smooth frequency slides between chords. The piece moves through 9 chords with varying durations, using JCRev reverb for spatial depth. Each voice glides independently to its next pitch, creating a rich, evolving harmonic texture.
