Sound Logo

A sound logo for the Stanford Mendicants, Stanford's oldest all-male a cappella group, where I serve as Music Director. The piece is based on a tag I arranged for the group, modeled after the chord progression of "All The Things You Are."

I transcribed my vocal arrangement into ChucK using 4 sawtooth oscillators (representing the 4 voice parts) with smooth frequency slides between chords to mimic the way voices glide between notes. JCRev reverb adds blend, mimicking the resonance of singing in a stairwell.
